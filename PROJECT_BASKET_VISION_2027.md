# Project Basket Vision 2027

## Vision
Project Basket is not just a website. It is a digital merchandising platform that helps customers build complete solutions while increasing basket size and gross profit.

## Customer Journey
A customer stops at Huron Market on the way home without a complete plan. Project Basket helps them decide what to buy for tonight.

## Guiding Philosophy
We are not selling products.
We are helping customers solve tonight.

## Digital Merchandising
Project Basket extends beyond the website through:
- Shelf talkers
- QR codes
- Cooler doors
- End caps
- Fuel pump signage
- Social media
- Email
- Digital displays
- Future POS integrations

## Recommendation Philosophy
Project Basket is not designed to convince customers to buy different products.
It is designed to help customers buy complete solutions.

## Customer Decision Points
Focus on helping customers while they are deciding:
- Spirits aisle
- Beer cave
- Wine section
- Grab & Go cooler
- Checkout
- Fuel pumps

## Long-Term Goal
Every customer leaves Huron Market with one more item than they originally planned because the platform genuinely helped them discover what they needed.

## Success Definition
Technology is successful only if it increases basket size, supports gross profit, improves the customer experience, or saves employee time.
