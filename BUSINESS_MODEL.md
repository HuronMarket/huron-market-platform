# Project Basket Business Model

## Core Principle
Destination products bring customers into Huron Market. Basket builders create the profit.

## Who We Are
Huron Market is a premium convenience market specializing in alcohol, fresh food, and tonight's meals.

We are not a full-line grocery store.

## Primary Business Goals
1. Increase alcohol-driven traffic.
2. Increase basket size.
3. Increase gross profit per transaction.
4. Save employees time.
5. Improve the customer experience.

## Destination Products
These bring customers into the store.
- Beer
- Retail Liquor
- Wine
- RTDs
- Gas
- Lottery

## Basket Builders
These naturally increase gross profit.
- Mixers
- Ice
- Chips
- Jerky
- Nuts
- Grab & Go
- Fresh Salads
- Deli
- Bakery
- Fresh Bread
- Fresh Produce

## Experience Builders
- Drink recipes
- Wine pairings
- Staff picks
- Seasonal recommendations
- New products

## Recommendation Strategy
Do not primarily recommend competing products.
Instead, recommend products that naturally complete the customer's purchase and increase basket size.

## Success Metrics
- Average basket value
- Items per transaction
- Basket lift
- Attachment rate
- Gross profit contribution
- Repeat visits

## Guiding Question
Every recommendation should answer:
"What else would genuinely help this customer today?"

## Mission
Use destination products to create opportunities for profitable basket growth while making Huron Market the easiest place to shop for tonight.
